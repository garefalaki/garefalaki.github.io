About

I am a PhD candidate in the Division of Evolutionary Biology at Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich. My research focuses on epigenetic variation and DNA methylation in natural populations, with particular interest in statistical frameworks for evaluating technical repeatability and heritability of methylation patterns.

Research

  • DNA methylation repeatability in natural populations
  • Epigenetic inheritance
  • Statistical analysis of high-dimensional biological data
  • Evolutionary genomics of wild bird populations

Publications
