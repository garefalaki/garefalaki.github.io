About

I am a PhD candidate in the Division of Evolutionary Biology at Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich. My research focuses on epigenetic variation and DNA methylation in natural populations, with particular interest in creating robust statistical frameworks for repeatability and heritability of methylation patterns.

Research

  • DNA methylation repeatability in natural populations
  • Epigenetic inheritance mechanisms
  • Statistical analysis of high-dimensional biological data
  • Evolutionary genomics of wild bird populations
